⚡ The Power of Bulk Auto-Fill

This is your "Secret Weapon." Instead of re-typing your company info into every bid or invoice, you set it once.



Navigate to Profile Setup in the Sidebar.



Enter your details (e.g., Company Name: Krispy Cleaning Professionals, Address: Grandview, MO).



Click Save Profile.



When you open any template, click the Auto-Fill button. The app will instantly swap placeholders like \[YOUR COMPANY NAME] with your saved data.
